What is Purpose:

According to Jeff Harter, Rebecca Cullers, Eduardo Rivero, from BCG BrightHouse team, purpose is your timeless why. It’s why your company exists and why it matters to the world. It’s a big deal. And if you’re articulating your company’s Purpose for the first time you have one shot to get it right. One opportunity to excavate it, articulate it, and bring it to life. It’s vital you find the right voice, strike the right tone, and inspire the right feeling. Why? Because the amount of care and effort you put into your Purpose rollout will signal to your employees how much effort you expect them to put into living the Purpose.
